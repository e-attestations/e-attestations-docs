To subscribe to one e-attestations API you have to **send and email*** to [api@eattestationscom.zohodesk.eu](mailto:api@eattestationscom.zohodesk.eu) 
or contact your e-Attestations account manager.

We advise you to discuss the configuration for your account concerning compliance documents with your account manager in order to fully understand your use case and advise you best.

Please send an email to [api@eattestationscom.zohodesk.eu](mailto:api@eattestationscom.zohodesk.eu) for any question and obtain your credntials.
